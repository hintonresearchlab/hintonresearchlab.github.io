We are proud to congratulate **Mrs. Pallavi Saikia**, a valued member of our research laboratory, on the successful defense of her PhD thesis.

Her doctoral research focused on **Alzheimer’s Disease Detection using Image Processing and Computer Vision Techniques**, addressing important challenges in medical image analysis and AI-assisted healthcare diagnostics. Throughout her research journey, she made significant contributions to the field through numerous research publications related to neuroimaging, deep learning, and intelligent disease detection systems.

Her work reflects a strong interdisciplinary approach combining artificial intelligence, medical imaging, and computer vision for early-stage neurological disorder analysis and diagnosis.

The entire lab extends heartfelt congratulations to Dr. Pallavi Saikia on this remarkable academic achievement and wishes her continued success in her future research and professional career.

We are delighted to announce that our Principal Investigator **Dr. Sanjib Kr. Kalita**, Associate Professor and Head of the Department of Computer Science, Gauhati University, has been awarded a prestigious research project funded by **ISRO-RAC-S**.

The project focuses on **Change Detection and Change Captioning using Computer Vision and Deep Learning Techniques**, aiming to develop intelligent AI-based frameworks for automated analysis and interpretation of remote sensing and satellite imagery data.

The project will involve advanced research in:
- Computer Vision
- Deep Learning
- Remote Sensing Image Analysis
- Automated Scene Understanding
- AI-driven Change Captioning Systems

The Co-Principal Investigators of the project are:

- **Dr. Diganta Kumar Pathak**, Assistant Professor, Department of Computer Science, Birangana Sati Sadhani Rajyik Vishwavidyalaya, Golaghat
- **Prof. Minakshi Gogoi**, Head, Department of Computer Science, Girijananda Chowdhury University, Guwahati

This project marks an important milestone for the research community and is expected to contribute significantly to intelligent geospatial analysis and AI-based remote sensing applications.

The entire laboratory and department congratulate the project team and wish them great success in this research endeavor.

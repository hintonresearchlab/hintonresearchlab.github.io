We are delighted to welcome **Manash Choudhuri** as a new member of our research laboratory at the Department of Computer Science, Gauhati University.

Manash has joined the lab as a **PhD Scholar** and **Junior Research Fellow (JRF)** under the **ISRO-RACS sponsored project** focusing on **Change Detection and Change Captioning** using advanced artificial intelligence and computer vision techniques.

He brings with him **two years of teaching experience** along with strong research interests in **Speech Processing**, **Deep Learning**, and intelligent data-driven systems. His expertise and academic background are expected to contribute significantly to the ongoing research activities of the lab.

The entire research group warmly welcomes him and wishes him great success in his research journey and future endeavors.

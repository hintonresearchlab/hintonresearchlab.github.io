A team from the Department of Computer Science, Gauhati University, actively participated in **Research Conclave 2024**, organized by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Girijananda Chowdhury University (GCU), Assam, on **28th November 2024**. The conclave was organized with the objective of fostering innovation, interdisciplinary research, and academic collaboration among researchers, students, and faculty members.

The research team comprised:

* **Deepjyoti Chetia**
* **Debasish Dutta**
* **Pallavi Saikia**
* **Rishiraj Baruah**
* **Himangshu Kashyap** (Master's Student)

The team presented research works spanning multiple domains of Artificial Intelligence and Computer Science, including:

1. **Image Segmentation using Transformer Architectures**
2. **Microscopy Image Super-Resolution using Transformer-based Models**
3. **Speech Processing using Deep Learning**
4. **Alzheimer’s Disease Detection using Computer Vision Techniques**
5. **DNA Sequencing and Bioinformatics Applications**

The event witnessed enthusiastic participation from researchers and academicians from various institutions. It provided an excellent platform for knowledge exchange, presentation of innovative ideas, and discussions on emerging technologies in Artificial Intelligence and Computing.

The Department is proud to announce that:

* 🏆 **Pallavi Saikia** received the **Best Paper Award** for her research work on Alzheimer's Disease Detection using Computer Vision.
* 🏆 **Himangshu Kashyap** received the **Best Paper Award** for his outstanding research presentation.

Their achievement reflects the quality of research being carried out within the department and highlights the growing impact of student and scholar-led research initiatives.

## The Department of Computer Science, Gauhati University, congratulates all the presenters and especially the award recipients for their outstanding accomplishment. Their success continues to inspire young researchers and reinforces the department's commitment to excellence in research and innovation.

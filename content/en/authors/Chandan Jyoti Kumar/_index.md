Dr. Chandan Jyoti Kumar is currently serving as an Associate Professor in the Department of Information Technology at Gauhati University, Guwahati, Assam. His research interests include Computer Vision, Agricultural Informatics, Bio-Signal Processing, Artificial Intelligence, Natural Language Processing, and AI applications in Mental Health.

He completed his M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from NIT Jalandhar and obtained his PhD from Gauhati University. He has extensive teaching and research experience in areas such as Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Digital Image Processing, and Pattern Recognition.

Dr. Kumar has published numerous research papers in reputed journals and conferences focusing on biomedical signal analysis, computer vision, deep learning, explainable AI, medical image analysis, and intelligent systems. His work also involves interdisciplinary research in healthcare AI, agricultural technologies, and intelligent data-driven systems.

He actively contributes to academic research supervision, AI-driven innovation, and collaborative interdisciplinary projects in emerging areas of artificial intelligence and computational intelligence.
